Molecular Informatics and Design Group integrates diverse disciplines of science and engineering in the design and development of advanced materials. Our approach to a research problem is ‘idea centric’ with a clear emphasis on the design phase, adopting modeling and informatics tools.



Research Areas: Network medicine, Bio-nano catalysis, Drug delivery
